THE MERCURY EXPERIENCE

What makes a journey to Mercury truly unforgettable

  • Double Sunrises

    Witness the rare phenomenon where the sun appears to rise, set, and rise again due to Mercury's unique orbit.

  • Crater Exploration

    Explore ancient impact craters with our guided tours, including the massive Caloris Basin.

  • Low-Gravity Activities

    Experience Mercury's 38% Earth gravity with our specially designed activities and sports.

  • Thermal Suit Experience

    Our state-of-the-art thermal suits allow you to safely experience Mercury's extreme temperatures.

  • Stellar Observations

    With no atmosphere to speak of, enjoy the clearest views of the cosmos ever seen by human eyes.

  • Historic Landing Sites

    Visit the landing sites of historic missions like MESSENGER and BepiColombo.
